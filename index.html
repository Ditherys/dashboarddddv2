KPI Score Guide

Reference rules based on the KPI scoring you provided.

KPI 1 2 3 4 5
Transfer Rate <3% 6% 9% 12% >15%
# of Admits 0 1 5 9 >13
AHT >04:09 03:32 02:56 02:20 <01:44
Attendance <90% 90-94% 95-99% - 100%
QA Score <95% 95-97% 98% 99% 100%

Performance = average of Transfer Rate, # of Admits, and AHT, then weighted at 50%. Attendance and QA are weighted at 25% each.
